Disney World : The Animal Kingdom

Our favorite area was Pandora!!

Amazing Experiences

  • You see the amazing Tree of Life as you enter the Animal Kingdom

  • Our favorite ride at Animal Kingdom is Avatar Flight of Passage

  • We enjoyed amazing food at the Tusker House. This was a Disney themed all you can eat buffet.

    • Tips if you decide to visit Animal Kingdom

  • Make sure if you are staying at one of the Disney resorts you use the early access. The most popular ride and our favorite will always have the longest line so make sure you use early access to get in line as early as possible.
  • If you would like to eat at one of the themed resturaunts. Make sure you make reservations on the disney app.
  • The biggest surprise we expereienced is Expedition Everest. I recommend everyone needs to ride this rollercoaster.
